## Examples

### Helper Text
<ComponentPreview
  client:load
  component='
    <breeze-textarea label="Message" helper-text="Please leave your message">
    </breeze-textarea>
  '
  style="display: block;"
/>

```html
<breeze-textarea label="Message" helper-text="Please leave your message"></breeze-textarea>
```

Or, using `slot` to add label and helper text if you need them to be HTML.

<ComponentPreview
  client:load
  component='
    <breeze-textarea>
      <p slot="label">Message</p>
      <p slot="helper-text">Please leave your message</p>
    </breeze-textarea>
  '
  style="display: block;"
/>

```html
<breeze-textarea>
  <p slot="label">Message</p>
  <p slot="helper-text">Please leave your message</p>
</breeze-textarea>
```
